RackTrack

Showcase Website — Design Reference

What is RackTrack?

A mobile app for data center technicians. You take a photo of a server rack, and the app uses AI to instantly identify every device, port, and cable inside it. It gives you a complete inventory, shows which ports are free, visualizes the rack in 2D and 3D, and syncs everything with your company's asset database.

One line: Point your phone at a rack → instantly know everything about it.

What the Showcase Website Should Highlight

These are the key selling points of RackTrack. The showcase website should communicate these to visitors.

  • 📸
    Scan a rack with your phone — Take a photo or use the live camera. The app tells you in real-time if the photo is good enough (sharp, well-lit, centered).
  • 🤖
    AI identifies everything automatically — Devices (switches, servers, routers, firewalls, UPS, etc.), ports, cables, and their colors. No manual data entry.
  • 🔍
    Reads device labels from the photo — OCR reads make, model, and serial number directly from the image. Then auto-fetches the full spec sheet from the vendor's website.
  • 📊
    Full rack inventory in seconds — Every device, every port, every cable. Expandable cards with connection status and cable colors.
  • 🔌
    Shows which ports are available vs. in use — Combines what the camera sees with live network data from the switch.
  • 🧊
    2D diagram and interactive 3D rack — Flat diagram for quick reference, plus a 3D model you can rotate, zoom, and click on.
  • 🌐
    Network neighbor discovery — See which device is plugged into which port. Highlights mismatches between physical and network data.
  • 🔄
    Syncs with ServiceNow CMDB — Compares scan vs. asset database, raises a change ticket, and pushes updates after approval.
  • 🛡️
    Firmware & vulnerability check — Compares current firmware vs. latest, scans for known security issues.
  • 🎯
    Link scans to open incidents — Pick an active ServiceNow incident before scanning, and the app focuses on the affected device.
  • 📄
    Reports & sharing — Export as HTML, CSV, or JSON. Share via Slack, Teams, or email.
  • 🌓
    Dark and light theme — Full dark mode support across the entire app.
